Conclusion
It it clear that IM technology is prevalent in modern society and will continue to have an impact it. For example, in 2015, IM was projected to grow to at least 3.8 billion accounts before 2020 (The Radicati Group). With the continued growth of IM usage in every sector, from businesses and schools to our personal lives, it is clear that there are many opportunities for IM companies to pursue desirable advancements in IM technology that will draw in more consumers. As more and more users flock to the convenience of instant messaging technology, the societal implications will be even more important to consider.
